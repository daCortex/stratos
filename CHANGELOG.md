# Changelog

**v1.1 — June 16, 2026**

✨ **New**
- Custom domains — point your own domain at your crew center; fully white-labeled with HTTPS, no "Stratos" in sight
- Infinite Flight integration — pilots verify their IFC account and pull their real grade & hours
- Live flight map — a real-time map of your pilots airborne on Infinite Flight
- Analytics dashboard — flights per month, pilot growth, top routes & top pilots
- Optional modules — switch the new power features on per airline from Settings → Modules

🔧 **Improved**
- Settings studio gains Modules and Domain tabs

Full details on bigger releases go in **#announcements**.

**v1.0 — June 16, 2026**

✨ **New**
- Multi-tenant platform — any virtual airline signs up and gets a fully-branded crew center
- Live customization studio — logo, colours, fonts, density, background and a menu builder
- Full crew center — roster, PIREP filing & review, ranks, leaderboards, news, LOA and reports
- Routes on a live map with SimBrief, events with sign-ups, awards & badges, Frequent Flyer points & rewards shop, challenges and NOTAMs
- Application-form builder with auto-graded exams
- Platform admin backend (Mission Control) — control every account and VA
- Help & guides center and a live Status page
- Neon Postgres persistence

🔧 **Improved**
- Minimal, modern UI with responsive navigation and larger, more readable text
